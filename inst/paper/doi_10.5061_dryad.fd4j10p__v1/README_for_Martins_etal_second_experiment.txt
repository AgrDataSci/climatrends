Data from Martins, A.A. Opedal, Ø.H., Armbruster, W.S. & Pélabon, C. 2019. Rainfall seasonality predicts the germination behaviour of a tropical dry-forest vine. Ecology and Evolution

Population = Population
Mother = Maternal plant ID
Cross_id = Unique cross identifier
Block = Experimental block
Treatment = Experimental treatment, given as time in days from seed dispersal to watering
newseedsize = Population-mean-centered seed mass in mm
